# Initial Concept
Queens Auto Services Algonquin: A specialized web platform for comprehensive auto repair and tire services in the Algonquin, IL area.

# Product Guide

## Overview
This project is a high-performance, SEO-optimized website built with Astro for "Queens Auto Services Algonquin". It serves as the primary digital gateway for customers in Algonquin and surrounding regions to access professional auto repair, maintenance, and tire services.

## Target Audience
- **Local Vehicle Owners:** Residents and commuters in Algonquin, Barrington, Carpentersville, Cary, and surrounding Illinois communities.
- **Maintenance Seekers:** Drivers looking for reliable auto repairs, including brakes, oil changes, and diagnostics.
- **Tire Service Customers:** Individuals needing new or used tires, installation, and balancing.

## Core Features
- **Online Booking System:** A streamlined interface for users to schedule service appointments and provide vehicle details.
- **Dynamic Service Catalog:** Context-aware pages for various auto repairs (brakes, diagnostics, electrical) and tire services.
- **Location-Specific Landing Pages:** Targeted content for cities within the service area to optimize local SEO.
- **Tire Selection Widget:** Interactive tools to help customers find the right tires for their vehicles.
- **Lead Generation:** Optimized call-to-action (CTA) elements designed to drive phone calls and form submissions.

## Success Metrics
- **Conversion Volume:** A steady increase in the number of qualified phone calls and completed appointment request forms.
- **Local Search Dominance:** High visibility in local search results for auto repair and tire services in the Algonquin service area.
